<div style="text-align: justify;" markdown="1">

My teaching focuses on equipping students with both the theoretical foundations of biomedical engineering and the practical computational skills required for medical data analysis.
I have served as an instructor for undergraduate courses in electronic engineering, digital signal processing, and medical imaging. 

In these roles, I have independently designed course materials, delivered lectures on theoretical concepts, and guided students through hands-on laboratory sessions.
My goal is to teach students how to effectively apply programming languages like MATLAB and Python to solve complex engineering and physiological problems.
